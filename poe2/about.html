About the Drop Chance Formula

Disclaimer: Please note that this information is speculative. While we have gathered data through datamining, much of it remains hidden and can only be identified based on patterns observed in the game. The numbers presented here are likely close to reality, as patterns tend to become clear over time, though exceptions can and do occur (for example, the drop rate of the Mirror of Kalandra is always an exception).

Factors Influencing Drop Chance

The formula behind the drop chance calculations is influenced by various factors, most notably the Increased Rarity Drop Rate. However, not all currencies interact with this modifier in the same way. Currencies like the Exalted Orb, Chaos Orb, and Divine Orb are more heavily affected by the Increased Rarity than others, even if their individual drop rates might appear low compared to these specific examples.

Diminishing Returns Effect

Additionally, a diminishing return effect has been observed once the total rarity reaches around 120, encompassing all sources including gear, maps, and modifiers. This explains why farming high-tier maps may not be as effective as one might expect, especially compared to grinding low-tier maps with a higher overall rarity setup.

Machine Learning Model

In order to create an accurate representation of these dynamics, I combined my own mathematical knowledge with the power of machine learning. Using a Python-based machine learning model, I was able to identify patterns, analyze data, and convert these insights into actionable numbers. The training of the model is similar to a form of engineering reverse applied to data. Instead of dismantling a physical object, the model "reverses" the hidden processes behind the drop system by learning from existing examples and inferring the rules that govern the behavior of the system. This method allows us to uncover complex relationships that are not explicitly documented but are observable through patterns in the data.

Data Sources

Due to the difficulty of finding clear exemplars and the time-consuming nature of producing data for the model to "study," I also gathered every piece of information available from Reddit and firsthand player reports. These reports included data from tests conducted with various rarity setups, including gear, maps, and tier adjustments.

Accuracy and Exceptions

The resulting calculations aim to reflect these complex relationships and provide a reliable estimate of your drop chances, but remember that as in any game, there may be occasional exceptions or changes to the pattern.
